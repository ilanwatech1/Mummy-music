About Mrs. Ruth Onoja

Mrs. Ruth Onoja, the esteemed president of The Voice of God music, embarked on her musical journey in 2000, guided by a profound divine inspiration. From the very beginning, her music has been a beacon of faith, hope, and encouragement.

With a career spanning over two decades, Mrs. Onoja has touched countless lives with her heartfelt songs. Her repertoire includes beloved tracks such as:

  • "Faithful God" – A song that celebrates the unwavering faithfulness of God.
  • "True Vine" – An inspiring piece that reflects the deep spiritual connection and nourishment found in divine love.
  • "Your Word" – A tribute to the power and guidance provided through divine teachings.

Her most recent album, "Dedication ODOBOGAWU", marks a significant milestone in her musical journey. This latest work is a profound dedication that continues to captivate and inspire her audience with its soulful melodies and powerful messages.

Mrs. Onoja's music is more than just a collection of songs; it's a heartfelt expression of her spiritual journey and a testament to her commitment to sharing her faith through her art. Each song she creates is infused with passion and purpose, aiming to uplift and inspire listeners around the world.
